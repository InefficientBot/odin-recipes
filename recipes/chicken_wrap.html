Mediterranean Chicken Wraps

Description

These healthy chicken wraps are filled with all the things most of us love!

Ingredients

  • Tortilla
  • Chicken
  • Hummus
  • Garlicky yogurt sauce
  • Avocado
  • Tomato
  • Cucumber

Steps

  1. Cook the chicken in a non-stick pan with spices and garlic.
  2. Prepare this everyday healthy salad.
  3. Mix the garlicky yogurt sauce.
  4. Blend some hummus (optional).
  5. Wrap it all together in a tortilla
